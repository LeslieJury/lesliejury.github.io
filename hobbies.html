My Hobbies

  • Programming Side Projects I like coding for fun, exploring new languages, and working on open-source software projects. I would like to eventually contribute more to open source projects I'm passionate for.
  • Crafts Crafts like making jewelry, painting, coloring, etc. help me relax and express creativity outside of engineering.
  • Videogames & Console Modding For fun I like to play videogames and modify gaming consoles to extend their functionality. I've modded a Nintendo 2DS, multiple PSPs, and a PSVita.
  • Collecting I enjoy collecting vintage electronics, retro game cartridges, anime figurines, comics/manga, and other nerdy memorabilia.
  • Digital Photography I have a vintage digital camera that I like to take on walks and trips to capture candid moments and practice photography.
  • Website Development As a beginner to the front-facing web development world, I am experimenting with my HTML skills on my personal Neocities website as well as this one, my more professional one.
