Displays (STM32 Nucleo)

In this project, I learned how to communicate to two different kinds of displays. In part A I learned how to use the PCD8544 and made my own dictionary of characters to access using the Char Creator in Google Sheets. In part B I learned how to display Hello World on the LCD 1602, I accessed functions to clear the screen and add text to the screen. In part C I used the skills gained in this lab in order to make an animation using the PCD 8544. This animation displays hearts and smiling faces going across the screen.
