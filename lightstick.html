Smart Lightstick (STM32 Nucleo)

In this project, I communicated using serial communication, I displayed messages using a display, and I controlled LEDs to follow a specific tempo of a song. I was inspired from concerts which often give their attendees lightsticks that flash to the beat or tempo of a song and even make optical illusions in the crowd, such as a wave with each lightstick in each row following a computer’s pre-programmed designs. These usually are controlled wirelessly, but for a good proof of concept, I showed a wired connection. I accomplished this through my own hard work and dedication. This was a wonderful learning experience for me and I hope you have as much fun watching my videos and reading this report as I had creating this wonderfully crafted project.
